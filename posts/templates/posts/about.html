About

And who are you?

Firstly, I am Farren, an actively learning and practicing web developer. I do concentrate on Django projects but I am also learning more and more about PHP and front-end frameworks as well. After separating from the Air Force I found that I needed to develop skills for what we call the "real world" as many skills from the Military did not bleed over. That is when I found Python. I made websites with HTML/CSS and JQuery during my younger days but once I found Python, GAME OVER. I was in love. So this blog is going to be following me along as I re-learn web development and see where this all takes me.
